## Q6
```python
y = int(input("Year: "))
m = int(input("Month: "))
d = int(input("Days: "))
h = int(input("Hours: "))
min = int(input("Minutes: "))

y = y * 365 * 24 * 60 * 60
m = m * 30 * 24 * 60 * 60
d = d * 24 * 60 * 60
h = h * 60 * 60
min = min * 60

sum = y + m + d + h + min

print(sum)
```

Alternative:

```python
y = int(input("Year: "))
m = int(input("Month: "))
d = int(input("Days: "))
h = int(input("Hours: "))
min = int(input("Minutes: "))

sum = (((y * 365 + m * 30 + d) * 24 + h) * 60 + min) * 60

print(sum)
```
